How it works
Three steps to a clean document

Works whether you're starting fresh or cleaning up an existing document.

1

Write LaTeX in your document

Type your formulas using standard LaTeX delimiters. Inline equations with $...$, centered display equations with $$...$$.

$E = mc^2$   $$\int_0^\infty e^{-x^2}dx$$
2

Open LaTeXFlow and scan

Go to Extensions → LaTeX Converter → Open Equation Panel. Switch to the Scan tab and click Scan Document. Every formula appears as a preview card.

3

Convert All — done

Hit Convert All. LaTeXFlow replaces every formula with a crisp image in place. Edit individual cards first if you need last-minute adjustments.
